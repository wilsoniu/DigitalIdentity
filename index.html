INTRODUCTION

In this project I designed a password system for iPhone in Three different stages ,The difficulty is increasing after each stage. This system is based on the idea of preparing and cooking a meal.

All the steps are animated and shown below.

STAGE ONE

PREPARATION

INSTUCTIONS

1.Tap on the food in the centre of the rings.

 

2.Drag the food to the right spot on the ring and release the item.

 

3.Repeat step one with the next three items.

 

4.Phone will be unlocked if all the position matches.

STAGE TWO

CUTTING

INSTUCTIONS

1.Select one food from the four at the bottom.

 

2.Slice the food in the right direction in the middle.

 

3.Select the next food you chose before and slice it like step 2.

 

4.Phone will be unlocked if all the food are sliced in the correct order and correct directions.

STAGE ONE

PREPARATION

INSTUCTIONS

1.Drag the correct food and the correct amount into the pot.

 

2.Set the amount of water you want to put.

 

3.Set the timer.

 

4.Phone will be unlocked if all the food, water amount and time are correct
